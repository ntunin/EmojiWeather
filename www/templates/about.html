Weather wiggy

Weather Wiggy will change the way you look at the weather!
This animated Weather widget will put the cool back into the forecast with several emoji like animations that change based on the weather conditions.
Get your up to date Weather Conditions, Time, 7-day forecast for your specific location all while enjoying the animated graphics.
Use the Menu button to navigate to the different screens.
Check out the weather in your favorite destination or a different country with the map and location feature.
You can change your Wiggy sets on the “Shop” screen.
